How to Nagivate?

To explore this project, follow these instructions to navigate through the different sections and elements:



Pan through the unrolled section
Use the navigation controls to move horizontally across the unrolled section. This allows you to explore the various areas and components of the project.

Double click to enter rooms of interest
When you come across a room or space that piques your curiosity, simply double click on it to enter and explore further. This will provide you with a more detailed view and a chance to immerse yourself in the design.

Rotate around the 3D model chunk
Take advantage of the interactive 3D model feature by rotating it. Use your cursor or device controls to rotate the model and gain different perspectives. This allows you to examine the project from various angles and appreciate its spatial qualities.

Flip through renderings and plans
Under the 3D model chunk, an image gallery of renderings and plans of the chunk. Flip through these visual representations to grasp the design intentions and visualize how different elements come together.


Feel free to engage with the project using these instructions, allowing you to navigate, explore, and appreciate the intricacies of the design. Enjoy the immersive experience and delve into the unique features of each section.
